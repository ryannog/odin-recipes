Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

Creamy southern baked mac and cheese recipe
It will take 1 hour and 20 minutes to prep and cook
Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour and 10 minutes
Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. (227 g) elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoon (30 ml) salted butter - plus more for baking dish
  • 1 large egg - beaten
  • 1 13 oz. can evaporated milk
  • 1½ cup (366 g) whole milk
  • 8 oz (227 g) extra-sharp white cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz (227 g) mild cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz (227 g) mascarpone cheese
  • 1½ tsp. (9 g) salt
  • 1 teaspoon (5 ml) cajun or creole seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon (2 ml) dry mustard powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon (1 ml) ground nutmeg
  • Fresh cracked pepper - for topping

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly coat a 3-quart casserole dish or 9x13 pan with butter or spray with cooking spray.
  2. Cook macaroni according to package directions, adding 2 minutes to the maximum time listed on the package. Drain; stir butter into macaroni until butter is melted.
  3. While macaroni is boiling, shred both cheese blocks using a box grater. Combine shredded cheese until evenly mix, then set aside about half of shredded cheese mixture for later.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together mascarpone and evaporated milk until smooth.
  5. Mix in the whole milk, followed by the salt, creole seasoning, mustard powder, and nutmeg. Stir in half of the shredded cheese mixture.
  6. Taste mixture to see if seasonings are to your preference and adjust if necessary. Stir in beaten egg.
  7. Add buttered macaroni to milk/cheese mixture and stir to combine. Pour the whole mixture into your prepared casserole dish.
  8. Sprinkle remaining shredded cheese evenly over the top, followed by some fresh cracked pepper, if you like.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until cheese on top is melted and browned, and filling is bubbling. Cool slightly before serving.
